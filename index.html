Gesture to Speech Conversion

This system converts the American sign language (ASL) into text and voice with the help of human gesture understanding and motion capturing. This project aims to lower the communication gap between the mute community and additionally the standard world. I've used Convolution neural network for the recognition and prediction of gestures.
